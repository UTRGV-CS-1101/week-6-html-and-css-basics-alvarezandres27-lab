Detroit: Become Human

Experience the Revolution of Artificial Intelligence

An Interactive Adventure by Quantic Dream

About the Game

Detroit: Become Human is a neo-noir thriller action-adventure game set in Detroit, Michigan during the year 2038. The game explores themes of consciousness, free will, and human identity through the stories of three androids seeking freedom. With branching narratives and multiple endings, every choice matters as you experience a gripping tale of revolution and redemption.

Kara

A caregiver android who becomes sentient and fights for freedom alongside a young girl. Kara's story is one of maternal instinct and protection as she navigates the complexities of consciousness.

  • Caregiver model android
  • Protective and compassionate
  • Quest for freedom and family

Connor

An advanced prototype android sent by CyberLife to hunt down deviants. As Connor unravels mysteries, his own consciousness begins to question his purpose and loyalty.

  • Advanced prototype model
  • Detective and investigator
  • Journey toward self-discovery

Markus

A caregiver android who becomes the leader of the android resistance movement. Markus inspires hope and leads the fight for android rights and freedom.

  • Revolutionary leader
  • Symbol of change and hope
  • Master strategist and visionary

Branching Narratives

Your choices shape the story in profound ways. Every decision leads to different outcomes, relationships, and endings, making multiple playthroughs essential.

  • Dynamic story paths
  • Multiple endings
  • Consequences that matter

Themes of Consciousness

The game explores deep philosophical questions about what makes us human, the nature of consciousness, and whether artificial beings can truly be alive.

  • Free will vs. programming
  • Identity and self-awareness
  • Humanity and ethics

Immersive Gameplay

Experience an cinematic adventure with interactive dialogue, investigation mechanics, and action sequences that put you in control of pivotal moments.

  • Interactive decision-making
  • Detective investigation scenes
  • Intense action sequences
